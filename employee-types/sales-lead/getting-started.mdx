Righthands handle the time-consuming research, outreach coordination, and follow-up tasks that keep your pipeline moving. This guide will help you understand how your Righthand can amplify your sales effectiveness and help you close more deals.

## Quick Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure Your ICP">
    Define your ideal customer profile, target industries, and qualification criteria for your Righthand to understand.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Your Tools">
    Integrate your CRM, email, and sales tools so your Righthand can access prospect data and update records.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start with Research">
    Begin by having your Righthand research and qualify a batch of leads to test accuracy and depth.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refine and Scale">
    Provide feedback on research quality and messaging, then scale up to full pipeline management.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Define Clear Qualification Criteria">
    The more specific your qualification criteria (company size, tech stack, buying signals), the better your Righthand can identify quality leads.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Provide Outreach Examples">
    Share examples of your best-performing emails and messages so your Righthand can match your tone and style.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Set Follow-up Cadences">
    Establish clear follow-up schedules (3-touch, 5-touch, etc.) and escalation paths for different prospect scenarios.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Review Before Sending">
    Always review personalized outreach and research summaries before they go to prospects to ensure accuracy.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
