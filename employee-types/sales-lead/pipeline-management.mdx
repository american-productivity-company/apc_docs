## Follow-up Strategy Framework

### Follow-up Timing Guidelines

**After Outreach Email:**
- Day 0: Initial email sent
- Day 3: Follow-up if no response
- Day 7: Second follow-up with different angle
- Day 14: Final "breakup" email

**After Discovery Call:**
- Hour 1-2: Send meeting summary and resources
- Day 3: Check in, ask if they have questions
- Day 7: Share relevant case study or content
- Day 10: Propose next steps / demo

**After Demo:**
- Hour 1-2: Send demo recording and highlights
- Day 2: Technical documentation or answers to questions
- Day 5: Pricing proposal or next steps
- Day 8: Follow up on proposal, offer call to discuss

**After Proposal Sent:**
- Day 2: "Did you receive the proposal? Any initial questions?"
- Day 5: "Happy to walk through the proposal together"
- Day 10: "What's your timeline for decision? How can I help?"
- Day 15: Escalate or move to nurture

<Warning>
Never let more than 7 days pass without contact during active deals. Momentum dies quickly.
</Warning>

## Best Practices

<Steps>
  <Step title="Daily Pipeline Review">
    Start each day by reviewing follow-up queue. Consistency beats intensity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Same-Day Follow-ups">

  <Step title="Regular Pipeline Cleaning">

  </Step>

## Metrics to Track

### Follow-up Effectiveness
- **Response Rate**: % of follow-ups that get replies (target: 30-40%)
- **Follow-up Timing**: Average time from event to follow-up (target: under 2 hours)
- **Follow-up to Meeting Rate**: % of follow-ups that result in next meeting
- **Persistence Payoff**: % of deals won after 5+ touchpoints

### Pipeline Health
- **Pipeline Velocity**: Average days from lead to close
- **Stage Conversion Rates**: % advancing from each stage to next
- **Deal Slippage**: % of forecasted deals that don't close on time
- **CRM Accuracy**: % of records with complete, current information

### Efficiency Gains
- **CRM Update Time**: Minutes saved on data entry per day
- **Follow-up Volume**: More consistent follow-ups without more effort
- **Pipeline Coverage**: Ratio of pipeline to quota maintained
- **Time to Close**: Impact of consistent follow-up on sales cycle length
