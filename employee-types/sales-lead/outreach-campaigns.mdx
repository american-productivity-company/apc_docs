## Overview

Effective outreach requires personalization at scale—researching each prospect, crafting relevant messaging, and maintaining consistent follow-up cadences. Righthands automate the research and personalization while keeping your outreach human and relevant.

## Outreach Best Practices

### Email Optimization

**Subject Lines That Work:**
```text Examples
❌ Generic: "Quick question"
✅ Specific: "Reducing data sync time at [Company]"

❌ Too sales-y: "Revolutionary solution for your team!"
✅ Value-focused: "How [Competitor] cut integration costs 60%"

❌ Vague: "Following up"
✅ Clear: "Thoughts on the data integration overview?"
```

**Email Structure:**
<Steps>
  <Step title="Hook (Line 1)">
    Reference specific detail about their company to prove you've done research
  </Step>
  <Step title="Problem (Lines 2-3)">
    Identify a pain point they likely have at their stage/industry
  </Step>
  <Step title="Solution (Lines 4-5)">
    Briefly explain how you solve it with proof point (case study, metric)
  </Step>
  <Step title="CTA (Last line)">
    Simple, low-commitment ask (15-min call, resource share, etc.)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Multi-Touch Email Sequences

Righthand creates consistent follow-up cadences with varied messaging:

```text Example 5-Touch Email Sequence
Day 0: Email #1 (Problem-focused with specific pain point)
Day 3: Email #2 (Solution-focused with case study)
Day 7: Email #3 (Different angle: cost savings or ROI)
Day 11: Email #4 (Social proof with customer testimonials)
Day 16: Email #5 (Breakup email with option to re-engage)
```

<Tip>
Varied messaging angles in each touch increase response rates. Test different value propositions to see what resonates.
</Tip>

### Sentiment Analysis & Response Prioritization

Righthand analyzes reply sentiment and prioritizes your response queue:

**High Priority** (Respond within 2 hours):
- Positive interest signals ("tell me more," "when can we talk?")
- Timing-specific ("free next Tuesday")
- Budget discussions

**Medium Priority** (Respond same day):
- Questions about features or pricing
- Referral to another team member
- Request for resources or case studies

**Low Priority** (Can batch respond):
- Generic "not interested" without specific objection
- "Touch base next quarter" without specific date
- Unsubscribe requests (automated handling)

## Metrics to Track

### Campaign Performance
- **Open Rate**: Target 20-30% for cold outreach, 40-50% for warm
- **Reply Rate**: Target 5-10% positive reply rate for cold outreach
- **Meeting Booked Rate**: Target 2-4% of outreach converts to meetings
- **Response Time**: Average time to respond to interested prospects (aim under 2 hours)

### Content Performance
- **Subject Line A/B Winners**: Track best-performing variations
- **Email Length Optimization**: Find sweet spot for your audience
- **CTA Conversion**: Which calls-to-action drive meetings vs. responses
- **Personalization Impact**: Lift from deeply personalized vs. templated

### Efficiency Gains
- **Time Saved**: Hours saved on research and message drafting per week
- **Volume Increase**: More prospects reached with same or less effort
- **Consistency**: Maintain cadence without manual tracking
- **Quality**: Response rates with AI assistance vs. manual outreach
