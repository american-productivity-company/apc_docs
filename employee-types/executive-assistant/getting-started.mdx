## As an Executive Assistant

Righthands manage the complex administrative tasks that keep you and your teams running smoothly. This guide will help you get started with your Righthand and understand how it can streamline your daily workflow.

## What Your Righthand Can Do For You

As an executive assistant, Righthand can help you with:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Calendar Management" icon="calendar" href="/employee-types/executive-assistant/calendar-management">
    Schedule meetings, resolve conflicts, and manage complex calendars
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email Triage" icon="envelope" href="/employee-types/executive-assistant/email-triage">
    Prioritize inbox, draft responses, and flag urgent messages
  </Card>
  <Card title="Meeting Coordination" icon="users" href="/employee-types/executive-assistant/meeting-coordination">

## Quick Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure Preferences">
    Configure working hours, calendar preferences, and communication style to match your needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Tools">
    Connect calendars, email, and other tools for the Righthand to manage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign Simple Tasks">
    Start by assigning straightforward tasks like scheduling a meeting or drafting an email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refine and Personalize">
    Provide feedback to help your Righthand learn preferences and improve performance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Provide Specific Instructions">
    The more context provided, the better your Righthand can perform. Include details like time zones, attendee priorities, and scheduling constraints.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Review Before Sending">
    Review calendar invites and emails before they're sent to ensure accuracy and appropriate tone.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Use Templates">
    Set up templates for common tasks like meeting scheduling or travel arrangements to improve efficiency.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
