## Overview

Hiring a Righthand takes less than two minutes. You'll choose a role, pick a work schedule, assign a manager, and your new AI employee will be up and running — complete with their own name, email address, and phone number.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:
- An active Righthand account with admin or manager access
- At least one team member to assign as the Righthand's manager

## Hiring Your Righthand

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Hire page">
    Navigate to the [Hire page](https://teams.righthand.ai/teams/hire) in the Righthand platform to begin the process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a role">
    Select the role you'd like to hire for. Each role comes preconfigured with relevant skills and integrations tailored to the position — from Executive Assistant to Software Engineer and beyond.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/hire/select-role.png" alt="Select a role for your Righthand" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a work schedule">
    Choose a pricing tier based on how much you need your Righthand to do:
    - **Part-Time** — Best for targeted tasks and overflow support. Your Righthand responds when called upon.
    - **Full-Time** — A dedicated team member that proactively handles their full role with enhanced intelligence.
    - **Over-Time** — Maximum output with 24/7 availability, advanced reasoning, and priority processing for your most demanding workflows.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/hire/select-tier.png" alt="Choose a work schedule for your Righthand" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign a manager and email domain">
    Select the team member(s) who will manage this Righthand — the first person selected becomes the primary manager. Then choose the email domain your Righthand will use for its email address.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/hire/assign-manager.png" alt="Assign a manager and email domain" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and hire">
    Click **Hire Righthand** and sit back. It takes less than a minute to provision your new AI employee.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're all set">
    Your Righthand will get set up and oriented on its own. Within 1–2 minutes, you'll receive a text message with their name, email address, and phone number. Once you receive it, they're ready to go.
  </Step>
</Steps>
