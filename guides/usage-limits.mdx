## Overview

A Righthand's usage capacity comes from their work schedule. If a Righthand reaches their limit, they may pause until more capacity is available or until their usage window resets.

To increase a Righthand's capacity, [change their work schedule](/guides/change-righthand-work-schedule) to a higher tier.

## How Limits Are Calculated

Usage is based on the work a Righthand performs. The Righthand platform summarizes this as hours worked for the current usage window.

## Viewing Your Usage

Open your team's billing or subscription view to see each Righthand's current tier, monthly cost, status, and hours worked. The billing table is a read-only overview.

To change a Righthand's tier, open the individual Righthand and go to **Settings**.

## Limit Tiers

Righthand work schedules are:

- **Part-Time** - Best for targeted tasks and overflow support. Your Righthand responds when called upon.
- **Full-Time** - A dedicated team member that proactively handles their full role with enhanced intelligence.
- **Over-Time** - Maximum output with 24/7 availability, advanced reasoning, and priority processing for your most demanding workflows.

## Increasing a Righthand's Capacity

To increase capacity, upgrade the Righthand's work schedule:

1. Go to [Righthands](https://teams.righthand.ai/righthands).
2. Open **People**, then select the Righthand.
3. Go to **Settings**.
4. In **Work schedule**, click the current **Tier** button.
5. Select the new tier and confirm the change.

## What Happens When Limits Are Reached

A Righthand that reaches a usage limit may pause and preserve pending work until more capacity is available or the relevant usage window resets.
