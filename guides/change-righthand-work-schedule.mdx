## Overview

A Righthand's work schedule controls their billing tier and usage capacity. You can change an existing Righthand between **Part-Time**, **Full-Time**, and **Over-Time** from that Righthand's Settings page.

The billing table shows each Righthand's current tier, cost, status, and hours worked, but it is a read-only overview. To change a tier, open the individual Righthand.

## Change a Work Schedule

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Righthands">
    Go to [Righthands](https://teams.righthand.ai/righthands) in the Righthand platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Righthand">
    Open **People**, then select the Righthand you want to update.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Go to the Righthand's **Settings** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Work schedule">
    In **Work schedule**, click the current **Tier** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the new tier">
    Select **Part-Time**, **Full-Time**, or **Over-Time**, then confirm the upgrade or downgrade.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Changes are prorated to your current billing cycle.

## Choosing a Tier

- **Part-Time** - Best for targeted tasks and overflow support. Your Righthand responds when called upon.
- **Full-Time** - A dedicated team member that proactively handles their full role with enhanced intelligence.
- **Over-Time** - Maximum output with 24/7 availability, advanced reasoning, and priority processing for your most demanding workflows.

## If You Do Not See Work Schedule

If the **Work schedule** section is not visible on the Righthand's Settings page, make sure you are viewing the individual Righthand, not the billing overview table. If it still is not visible, contact [support@humans.righthand.ai](mailto:support@humans.righthand.ai).
