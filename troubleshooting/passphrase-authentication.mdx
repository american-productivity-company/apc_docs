---
title: "Passphrase Authentication Issues"
description: "What happens if your Righthand can't authenticate you by passphrase."
icon: "lock-keyhole"
---

If your Righthand can't verify your passphrase, it will pause any sensitive action and ask you to try again. This keeps requests secure and prevents unintended changes.

## Try these steps

1. Enter the passphrase exactly as you set it (watch for capitalization, spaces, and punctuation).
2. Make sure you’re using the current passphrase for this Righthand.
3. Retry in a new chat if the conversation looks out of sync.

## Still stuck?

If authentication keeps failing, contact support and include the task you were trying to run. We’ll help you re-verify your access.
